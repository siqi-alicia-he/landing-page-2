Market Background & Pain Points

Why now

  • Rising flexible work adoption
  • Remote collaboration & online delivery become default
  • Digital skill outsourcing is surging

Who needs it

  • SMEs, startups, agencies (ads, software, PR)
  • Professionals & side-hustlers
  • Students seeking paid, portfolio-building work

Current gaps: non-transparent pricing, poor pre-brief info, high coordination cost, weak risk control, and survival barriers for new/individual sellers.

Market Opportunity

China’s SMEs account for ~96.5% of market entities, contribute ~60% of GDP and ~80% of employment. Adjacent markets (self-media marketing, advertising, social commerce) are large and growing.

Product & Core Flow

  1. Publish a Skill (structured, standardized)
  2. Search & Match (tagged, ranked, multi-signal relevance)
  3. Secure Order (escrow, staged payments)
  4. Deliver (clear artifacts, timeline, revision limits)
  5. Close or Resolve (dispute support)
  • Standardized skill cards → clear scope & expectations
  • Rank by ads | online | rating | relevance | newness
  • Set revision limits & deliverable specs up front
  • Escrow & staged payouts → risk control for both sides

Key Features

Transparent Pricing

Show historical average deal prices; clear rate cards where possible.

Better Matching

Structured tags, richer bios, portfolio proofs, and multi-signal rankings.

Workflow Safety

Escrow, staged milestones, documented deliverables, and dispute aids.

Competitive Landscape

Direct: short-cycle, lower ticket marketplaces (e.g., skill retail verticals).
Indirect: long-cycle, higher ticket BPO-style platforms.

Job Me focuses on skill retail with flow tailored to skills, fairer ranking logic, and safer transactions.

Business Model

  • Commission: ~10% (early), tiered later
  • Category slots: one free, extras paid
  • Ads: traffic boost with guardrails
  • Tools: large-file transfer, advanced collaboration
  • Financial: escrow float income
  • Solution bundles for common project types

Progress

  • Product: skill posting, search/filter, order & delivery flow implemented
  • Research: 500+ questionnaires, 20+ interviews, 5+ courses & coaching
  • Supply: campus community 500+ members in a week, 6 categories covered, 200+ tasks gathered
  • Demand: 100+ campus requests, 10+ studio/agency LOIs, 2,000+ orders/month (target environment)

Roadmap

0–6 months

Campus-first, warm brand tone, “light part-time” concept; penetrate Shanghai universities.

6–12 months

Mature website & mini-program; paid ads, KOLs, social growth; 100k+ users goal.

12–24 months

SEO/SMO/PR, pop-ups & city activations; expand to freelancers & higher-end gigs.

3–5 years

Community & CRM; “Pro” collaboration; data-driven matching; China’s most complete skills job graph.

Close info gaps, increase income, choose when & where to work.

Nation

Support training, increase tax base, reduce unemployment; serve 1M+ freelancers indirectly.
